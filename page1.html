For any questions regarding how to program your radio, please refer to the instruction manual for your particular radio model. If you are having trouble understanding, please join our discord server and one of the members there should be more than happy to assist.

The best GMRS repeater in the Orlando area is going to be the Orlando 700. You can find the frequencies and privacy tones on the GMRS Repeater Map linked above.

There is a large UHF machine in Orlando that is connected via the internet to the East Coast Reflector. It can be found using the UHF/VHF Repeater list on the top of this page, and the frequency is 444.800

SARNet is a great way to communicate across the state of Florida without any internet connection. Please yield for any emergency communications on this system and do not ragchew during times of emergency, such as hurricanes or other large scale emergencies.

If you're bored or looking to explore outside of VHF/UHF you can listen to a large portion of the frequency spectrum through your internet browser with a public SDR receiver. You can find a map of them across the entire globe here.
